Welcome to Extension Services Program

Capacitating community and its people through trainings, workshops and knowledge transfer as an extension service.

Extension services are the vehicles that link the major sectors of society for the purpose of developing and enhancing capabilities of inidividuals and groups (POs, NGOs) to aleviate their socio-economic conditions. Through extension and community outreach programs of the State University, learning beyond classrooms may have a new meaning, as modalities shall be tested to develop and enhance capabilities of the Caraganons.

Extension Services Objectives

The Extension and Community Outreach Service endeavors to:

  • Reduce poverty and generate employment through the effort in packaging, disseminating and utilizing technologies for rural development;
  • Serve individuals and communities through extension service programs in Sustainable Upland or Low Land Resource Development, Responsible Mining, Human Capital Building Through Improving Literacy Technical and Vocational Courses, Engineering, Education and Other Fields for Practical Applications; and
  • Attain Excellence in Community Service

      • Improving indigenous and rural technology

      Supporting provincial and regional development directions through linkages with NGOs, LGUs and other sectors;

      Cultivating values and culture toward attaining improved quality of life in rural communities;

      Playing major role in the making of important and relevant policies;

      Training the lower-and-middle level manpower in agriculture, wood and mineral-based industries and other related endeavors;

      Conducting outreach programs on literacy and occupational skills for various sectors in the service area.
